About

The people who need AI most are the ones who can trust it least.

Kennedy Applied Sciences combines PhD-level research in AI, cognitive augmentation, and neuromorphic computing with over 24 years of collective leadership in healthcare IT and enterprise operations. The team holds PMP certification and advanced degrees in Economics and Marketing.

That background produced StratoSort Core — privacy-first, AI-native software built to meet HIPAA, FINRA, and SOX compliance requirements. Every model runs on-device. No data is transmitted. The architecture assumes no network connectivity, not as a fallback, but as the design requirement.

We build for professionals in healthcare, legal, and financial services — industries where data sovereignty is the entire point.
